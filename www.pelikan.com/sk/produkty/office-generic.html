Handifilm 205®

The Handifilm 205® carbon paper from Pelikan is immediately smear proof and writes in blue. It has a special foil with plastic ink coating.

  • DIN A4 100 sheets

Ultrafilm 410®

The Ultrafilm 410® carbon paper is thermo-copyable and especially ideal for documents. It also has a high-coverage plastic ink coating.

  • Folio 100 sheets
